How to get from Incheon Airport to Myeongdong cheaply

JK

James K.

Mar 27, 2026 1,205 24

After arriving at Incheon International Airport, one of the first challenges every tourist faces is getting to the city center. I've tried all three main options and here's my honest breakdown.

Option 1: AREX (Airport Railroad Express)

The AREX Express train runs directly from Incheon Airport to Seoul Station in about 43 minutes. The fare is ₩9,500 (about $7 USD). From Seoul Station, you can transfer to the subway to reach Myeongdong in about 10 more minutes.

💡 Pro tip: The All-Stop AREX train costs only ₩4,750 but takes about 56 minutes. Great if you're not in a rush!

Option 2: Airport Limousine Bus

Airport buses run every 15-20 minutes and cost between ₩15,000-₩17,000. Bus 6015 goes directly to Myeongdong. The ride takes about 60-80 minutes depending on traffic. Very comfortable with luggage space.

Option 3: Taxi

A taxi to Myeongdong costs around ₩65,000-₩85,000 depending on traffic and time of day. Only worth it if you're splitting with a group of 3-4 people. Make sure to use the official taxi stand.

My Recommendation

For solo travelers or couples, the All-Stop AREX + subway transfer is the best value. Total cost under ₩6,000 and you avoid traffic entirely. For groups or if you have heavy luggage, the airport bus is your best bet.
